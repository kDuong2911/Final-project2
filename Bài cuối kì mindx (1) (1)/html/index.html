Event timeline

1940

France, current Vietnam's colony fell into Germany's hand. Japan soon take over, allowing little control for France

1941

Seeing an opportunity to free Vietnamese, Ho made his way from Vietnam to China. He and his few initial followers mostly operate in the mountainous jungles between the border of Vietnam and China

March 1945

The war in the Pacific of Vietnam clearly turned against Japan, Tokyo seized direct control of Vietnam and evicted French's troops. Taking advtange of the confusion, Ho's forces, Viet Minh, take over even more territory. Seeing the potential of Vietnam after the success, OSS, the forerunner of CIA decided to make a deal with Ho. In March 1945, an officer of OSS made a deal with Ho, promissing him to equip his forces with even more weapons in exchange of intelligence as well as try to rescue pilots who were shot down in Viet Minh - controlled territory

September 2 1945

After Japanese troops surrendered in mid-August 1945. A crowd of thousands of people gathered at Ba Dinh's square to hear Ho declaring Vietnam's independance
